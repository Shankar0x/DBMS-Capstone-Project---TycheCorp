Tyche Corp


Tyche Corp. is an Asset Management Company (AMC) which invests funds pooled by its customers in various sectors like stock, bonds, mutual funds, real estate, bank loans, fixed deposits, capital for start-ups etc. Through these varied options, the investments will be diversified into different services to maximize the net profit for the investment made by the customer. A customer can select one or multiple services which they deem would be the best option for investing and Tyche Corp. enables them to do so. Tyche Corp. is a platform that provides various investment options and creates portfolios for customers displaying information about their profits and losses across all services just in a single click! Also, Tyche Corp. provides customers with information about new and trending stocks and schemes. The customers have the flexibility of changing from one scheme to another of their choice. Inbuilt allowance limiter enables customer to set a limit to the money being spent for that particular month. The notification system keeps track of all the investments made and due amount of each of them will be notified on a monthly basis.
